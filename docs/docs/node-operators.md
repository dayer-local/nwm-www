# Node Operators
*Who's on the mesh, and roughly where*

---

!!! info "Information"
    This website is very beta. Many pages are broken or they just don't exist. Please be patient, I am only one person. Thanks!

This is a list of the people running nodes on the New Welsh Mesh. It's not a tracking map, you decide what you put here. A rough area is plenty, nobody needs your exact location.

If your node's on the air, add yourself. It helps everyone know who's about, where the coverage roughly is, and who to give a shout if something needs looking at.

## How to add yourself
1. Click the edit button, the pencil icon in the top right of this page.
2. Add a row to the table below with your node's details.
3. Send it over. Once it's had a quick look it'll show up here.

Not comfortable editing the page? No problem, drop your details in the [Discord](https://discord.gg/4VhKqbH39W) and we'll add you.

!!! tip "Keep it sensible"
    Share as much or as little as you like. A node name and a rough area, town or valley, is all that's needed. Don't go putting your home address on a public web page.

## What goes in each column
* **Node Name** — the long name shown in the Meshtastic app.
* **Short** — your 4-character short name.
* **Area** — rough location. A town or valley, not your postcode.
* **Type** — Client, Router (a repeater), or Router_Client.
* **Notes** — anything handy. Solar-powered, always on, portable, that sort of thing.
