# Get Involved
*The mesh only works if people run nodes*

---

!!! info "Information"
    This website is very beta. Many pages are broken or they just don't exist. Please be patient, I am only one person. Thanks!

The New Welsh Mesh isn't a company or a product. It's a group of people running radios so we can talk without relying on the phone networks. It only grows if people get involved, so here's how you can help.

## Run a node
The most useful thing you can do is put a node on the air. Every node that joins makes the mesh bigger and more reliable for everyone. You don't need much, a £20 Heltec V3 on a windowsill works fine.

If you haven't got a device yet, have a look at [Node Choices](node-choices.md) to pick one, then follow the [Setup Guide](getting-started.md) to get it on the mesh.

Once you're up and running, add yourself to the [Node Operators](node-operators.md) list so the rest of us know you're there.

## Run a repeater
If you've got somewhere high up with a bit of power spare, a loft, a roof, the top of a hill, a fixed repeater helps coverage a lot. One well-placed repeater can connect two parts of the mesh that couldn't reach each other before.

There's a full guide in [Node Configuration](node-settings.md) under the repeater section. If you set one up, get in touch and we'll list it.

## Help out on Discord
Most of the day-to-day chat happens on the [Discord](https://discord.gg/4VhKqbH39W). Answering setup questions, welcoming new people, or just letting everyone know your node is live all helps. If you'd like to help keep things running, we could use a hand moderating too.

[Join the Discord](https://discord.gg/4VhKqbH39W){ .md-button .md-button--primary }

## Fix the website
Spotted a typo, a dead link, or something out of date? You can fix it yourself. Every page has an edit button, the pencil icon in the top right, that opens the page on GitHub. Make your change and send it over, and it'll come through for review.

Not comfortable with GitHub? No problem, just mention it on the Discord and we'll sort it. There's a [contributing guide](https://github.com/dayer-local/nwm-www/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) on the repo if you want the details.

## Spread the word
The mesh gets more useful the more people are on it, especially close together. Tell your mates, your local makerspace, anyone who might be into it. The more nodes around Tonyrefail and across the valleys, the better it works for all of us.
